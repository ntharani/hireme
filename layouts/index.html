There are two paths to being a PM that I've found:

  1. Starting out at a large company and learning a variety of roles in a compressed time frame
  2. Having worked in a variety of roles that combined gives you a deep appreciation and respect of all business functions. Although I'm an engineer by training, I've done sales, business development, program management and customer service over the course of my career. I also actively code on the side.
And 3 crazy important traits:
